About Us

COVIDTutor is a Peer-to-Peer tutoring network created to help supplement the education of students in the United States. Due to the spread of Covid-19, schools across the country have converted to online learning systems. We are a way for students to get help with their school work while providing their peers with volunteer hours.  

1:1 Support 

This is a personal approach to a system that can leave students confused and falling behind. Students are able to get help from students like themselves.

Volunteer Hours

Finding volunteer hours can be very difficult at this time. Remote tutoring your peers allows you to stay healthy while gaining crucial volunteer hours.

Countless Subjects

Tutors ready to help you with anything from SAT English to AP U.S. Government to Algebra II.
